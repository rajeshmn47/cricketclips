# CricketClips

CricketClips is a cricket video search and retrieval platform designed to help fans, analysts, coaches, and content creators quickly find specific deliveries, shots, wickets, and moments from cricket matches.

Instead of manually watching hours of match footage, users can search and filter for precise cricket events.

## Features

### Ball-by-Ball Search

Search for deliveries using filters such as:

* Bowler
* Batter
* Match
* Tournament
* Team
* Over
* Innings

### Event-Based Search

Find deliveries based on:

* Wickets
* Dot balls
* Boundaries
* Sixes
* Fours
* Extras

### Bowling Analysis

Search for specific bowling events:

* Seam movement
* Swing movement
* Yorkers
* Bouncers
* Inswingers
* Outswingers

Example:

> Show every Mohammed Shami delivery with significant seam movement.

### Batting Analysis

Search for:

* Cover drives
* Pull shots
* Hook shots
* Cut shots
* Straight drives
* Lofted shots

Example:

> Show every Virat Kohli cover drive boundary against Australia.

### Commentary Integration

Link deliveries with:

* Ball-by-ball commentary
* Match context
* Score information

### Video Retrieval

Instantly retrieve video clips matching search criteria.

Examples:

* Every Bumrah wicket in IPL finals
* Every Starc inswinger resulting in a wicket
* Every Kohli boundary in successful run chases

## Use Cases

### Fans

Relive memorable moments without searching through entire matches.

### Analysts

Study player performance, bowling patterns, and batting techniques.

### Coaches

Review specific deliveries and shots for training and performance analysis.

### Content Creators

Quickly locate clips for cricket-related content and storytelling.

## Future Roadmap

## Vision

The long-term goal of CricketClips is to make cricket searchable at the delivery level, allowing users to instantly discover and explore specific moments from the sport's vast video archive.
